## KEBIJAKAN PRIVASI (BAHASA INDONESIA)

Hegemoni Lex ("Kami," "Kami," atau "Perusahaan") berkomitmen untuk melindungi privasi Anda. Kebijakan Privasi ini menjelaskan bagaimana kami mengumpulkan, menggunakan, mengungkapkan, dan melindungi informasi Anda saat Anda mengakses situs web kami (hegemonilex.com) dan layanan terkait.

### 1. INFORMASI YANG KAMI KUMPULKAN

#### 1.1 Informasi Langsung
- **Akun Pengguna:** Nama, email, password (terenkripsi), nomor telepon (opsional)
- **Konten Pengguna:** Komentar, rating, ulasan artikel yang Anda posting
- **Pendaftaran Penulis:** Nama penulis, CV, portofolio hukum (untuk penulis/kontributor)

#### 1.2 Informasi Otomatis
- **Data Teknis:** IP address, browser type, sistem operasi, referrer pages
- **Cookies:** ID sesi, preferensi bahasa, history browsing di situs kami
- **Analytics:** Waktu kunjungan, durasi, halaman yang dilihat (via Google Analytics 4)
- **Device Fingerprinting:** Hashed fingerprint dari userAgent + timezone (untuk menghitung unique visitors)

#### 1.3 Informasi Pihak Ketiga
- **File Upload:** Jika Anda mengunggah foto profil atau artikel, kami menyimpannya di Supabase Storage
- **Email Verification:** Email provider (Gmail, Yahoo, etc.) digunakan untuk autentikasi NextAuth

### 2. BAGAIMANA KAMI MENGGUNAKAN INFORMASI

#### 2.1 Tujuan Utama
- Memberikan, memelihara, dan meningkatkan layanan kami
- Memproses registrasi akun dan autentikasi
- Menyelenggarakan konten (artikel, komentar, ulasan)
- Mengirim notifikasi penting terkait akun Anda
- Menganalisis tren pengguna dan performa konten

#### 2.2 Marketing & Komunikasi
- **Email:** Kami dapat mengirim newsletter mingguan tentang artikel terbaru (jika Anda opt-in)
- **Social Media:** Kami dapat membagikan konten Anda di LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter dengan atribusi
- **Improvement:** Kami menggunakan feedback Anda untuk memperbaiki fitur produk

#### 2.3 Kepatuhan & Keamanan
- Mematuhi hukum Indonesia (UU Perlindungan Data Pribadi, UU ITE)
- Mencegah fraud, abuse, atau pelanggaran ToS
- Merespons permintaan hukum dari pegawai negeri

### 3. SIAPA YANG MEMILIKI AKSES KE INFORMASI ANDA

#### 3.1 Karyawan Hegemoni Lex
- Admin: Dapat melihat email, nama, konten publik
- Moderator: Dapat melihat komentar untuk review approval
- Analytics: Dapat melihat aggregate data (tidak individual)

#### 3.2 Penyedia Layanan (Subprocessors)
| Penyedia | Penggunaan | Lokasi |
|---|---|---|
| **Supabase** | Database PostgreSQL + File Storage | Singapore/Global |
| **Vercel** | Hosting aplikasi Next.js | US |
| **Google Analytics** | Analytics tracking | US |
| **NextAuth (Auth0 optional)** | Autentikasi email | Global |
| **Nodemailer/SendGrid** | Email transaksional | Global |

#### 3.3 Pihak Lain
- **Pemerintah Indonesia:** Jika diperintahkan oleh pengadilan atau aturan hukum
- **Penulis/Kontributor:** Jika Anda adalah penulis, profil Anda publik (dapat diakses pembaca)
- **Pembaca:** Komentar dan rating Anda ditampilkan ke publik (kecuali dihapus moderator)

### 4. RETENSI DATA

- **Akun Aktif:** Data disimpan selama akun Anda aktif
- **Akun Dihapus:** Data dihapus dalam 30 hari (kecuali diperlukan untuk kepatuhan hukum)
- **Cookies:** Dihapus saat Anda logout atau 1 tahun inaktif
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics menyimpan data 14 bulan (retention setting default)
- **Backup:** Kami menyimpan backup database untuk disaster recovery (disimpan hingga 30 hari)

### 5. KEAMANAN DATA

- **Enkripsi:** Password dienkripsi bcrypt (tidak disimpan plaintext)
- **HTTPS:** Semua transmisi data menggunakan SSL/TLS
- **File Upload:** Discan untuk malware sebelum penyimpanan
- **Access Control:** IP allowlist untuk admin dashboard
- **Audit Logs:** Semua admin actions dicatat untuk transparansi

Kami tidak dapat menjamin keamanan 100%, tetapi kami mengikuti standar industri best practices.

### 6. COOKIE & TEKNOLOGI TRACKING

**Cookies yang kami gunakan:**
| Nama | Tujuan | Durasi | Diperlukan |
|---|---|---|---|
| `sessionId` | Sesi login | 24 jam | Ya |
| `token` | NextAuth bearer token | 30 hari | Ya (jika login) |
| `ga4_*` | Google Analytics | 2 tahun | Tidak (opt-in kami via cookie consent) |
| `language` | Preferensi bahasa | 1 tahun | Tidak |

**Teknologi Tracking Lainnya:**
- Device Fingerprinting (untuk menghitung unique daily visitors; tidak personally identifiable)
- UTM parameters (untuk tracking sumber traffic)

## PRIVACY POLICY (ENGLISH VERSION)

Hegemoni Lex ("We," "Us," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you access our website (hegemonilex.com) and related services.

### 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

#### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **User Account:** Name, email, password (encrypted), phone number (optional)
- **User Content:** Comments, ratings, article reviews you post
- **Writer Registration:** Author name, CV, legal portfolio (for writers/contributors)

#### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, referrer pages
- **Cookies:** Session ID, language preference, browsing history on our site
- **Analytics:** Visit time, duration, pages viewed (via Google Analytics 4)
- **Device Fingerprinting:** Hashed fingerprint from userAgent + timezone (for counting unique visitors)

#### 1.3 Third-Party Information
- **File Upload:** If you upload profile photos or articles, we store them in Supabase Storage
- **Email Verification:** Email providers (Gmail, Yahoo, etc.) used for NextAuth authentication

### 2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

#### 2.1 Primary Purposes
- Provide, maintain, and improve our service
- Process account registration and authentication
- Manage content (articles, comments, reviews)
- Send account-related notifications
- Analyze user trends and content performance

#### 2.2 Marketing & Communication
- **Email:** May send weekly newsletter with latest articles (if opted-in)
- **Social Media:** May share your content on LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter with attribution
- **Improvement:** Use your feedback to enhance product features

#### 2.3 Compliance & Security
- Comply with Indonesian law (Personal Data Protection Law, ITE Law)
- Prevent fraud, abuse, or ToS violations
- Respond to lawful government requests

### 3. WHO HAS ACCESS TO YOUR INFORMATION

#### 3.1 Hegemoni Lex Employees
- Admin: Can view email, name, public content
- Moderator: Can view comments for approval review
- Analytics: Can view aggregate data (not individual-level)

#### 3.2 Service Providers (Subprocessors)
| Provider | Use | Location |
|---|---|---|
| **Supabase** | PostgreSQL Database + File Storage | Singapore/Global |
| **Vercel** | Next.js App Hosting | US |
| **Google Analytics** | Analytics Tracking | US |
| **NextAuth/Auth0** | Email Authentication | Global |
| **Nodemailer/SendGrid** | Transactional Email | Global |

#### 3.3 Third Parties
- **Indonesian Government:** If ordered by court or legal requirement
- **Writers/Contributors:** If you are a writer, your profile is public
- **Readers:** Your comments and ratings are displayed publicly (unless moderator deletes)

### 4. DATA RETENTION

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data deleted within 30 days (unless legally required)
- **Cookies:** Deleted at logout or 1 year of inactivity
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics retains data for 14 months (default setting)
- **Backups:** Database backups retained for 30 days (disaster recovery)

### 5. DATA SECURITY

- **Encryption:** Passwords encrypted with bcrypt (never stored plaintext)
- **HTTPS:** All data transmission encrypted with SSL/TLS
- **File Uploads:** Scanned for malware before storage
- **Access Control:** IP allowlist for admin dashboard
- **Audit Logs:** All admin actions logged for transparency

We cannot guarantee 100% security, but we follow industry best practices.

### 6. COOKIES & TRACKING TECHNOLOGY

**Cookies we use:**
| Name | Purpose | Duration | Required |
|---|---|---|---|
| `sessionId` | Login session | 24 hours | Yes |
| `token` | NextAuth bearer token | 30 days | Yes (if logged in) |
| `ga4_*` | Google Analytics | 2 years | No (opt-in via cookie consent) |
| `language` | Language preference | 1 year | No |

**Other Tracking Technology:**
- Device Fingerprinting (for counting unique daily visitors; not personally identifiable)
- UTM parameters (for tracking traffic source)

### 7. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

**7.1 Access**
- Request a copy of your personal data. Contact: privacy@hegemonilex.com

**7.2 Correction**
- Request us to correct inaccurate data
- Self-update via account dashboard

**7.3 Deletion**
- Request account & data deletion (right to be forgotten)
- We will delete within 30 days, unless legal reason applies

**7.4 Objection**
- Object to certain data processing
- We will review within 14 days

**7.5 Restricted Processing**
- Request data processing be restricted (if in legal dispute)

**To Submit a Request:** Email privacy@hegemonilex.com with:
- Full name
- Registered email
- Description of request
- Proof of identity (ID card scan, minimum)

We will respond within 5 business days.

### 8. POLICY CHANGES

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you:
- Email to all registered users (if material changes)
- Banner on homepage (30 days notice)
- Update date "Effective Date" on this page

Continued use means you accept changes. If you disagree, use your right to delete.

### 9. GDPR & INTERNATIONAL REGULATIONS

If you're an EU user:
- We don't actively target EU, but if you access our site, GDPR applies
- Our legal basis: Consent (cookies) + Legitimate interest (analytics)
- Our data processors (Supabase, Vercel) have Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
- You have right to lodge complaint with your local data protection authority

### 10. CONTACT

For privacy inquiries:

**Email:** privacy@hegemonilex.com
**Mail:** Hegemoni Lex, Jakarta, Indonesia
**Response Time:** 5 business days
