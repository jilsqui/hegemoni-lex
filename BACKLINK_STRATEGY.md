## BACKLINK QUALITY CHECKLIST

When evaluating if a placement is worth it:
- [ ] Backlink is **dofollow** (not "nofollow") - verify with Ahrefs
- [ ] Domain authority 20+ minimum (community links okay, but prioritize DA 30+)
- [ ] Relevant anchor text (e.g., "hukum ketenagakerjaan" instead of "click here")
- [ ] Your author bio includes byline link (not hidden in footer)
- [ ] Publication is indexed by Google (check: site:targetmedia.com)

**Red flags to avoid:**
- ❌ Private blog network (PBN) - destroys SEO
- ❌ Requires payment/cross-link ("we'll post if you post ours") - violates AdSense
- ❌ Link buried in unrelated article (relevance matters)
- ❌ No author bio visible (wasted opportunity)

## QUICK-WIN TACTICS (Start Immediately)

### 1. LinkedIn Connections + Engagement
- Connect dengan legal journalists, law bloggers, KOL lokal
- Like + comment on their posts weekly
- Send DM: "Love your article on [topic]. Hegemoni Lex just published similar piece..."
- 30% conversion to collaboration or backlink mention

**Time investment:** 10 min/day
**Expected ROI:** 2-3 organic backlinks + 50-100 referral clicks/month

### 2. Quora + Reddit Participation
- Answer 2-3 questions daily on Quora Indonesia
- Each answer can include 1 relevant link to article
- Upvote relevant, link to your article as "I covered this in detail here..."

**Time investment:** 15 min/day
**Expected ROI:** 100-150 monthly referral visitors (lower authority, but high intent)

### 3. Email Outreach to Niche Influencers
- Identify 10 legal bloggers, law school alumni with blog, business journalist
- Email: "I loved your piece on X. Hegemoni Lex analyzed similar topic with different angle here [link]"
- Ask: "Any chance you'd mention in future piece?"
- 20% chance they share/backlink organically

**Time investment:** 30 min (one-time setup)
**Expected ROI:** 3-5 organic backlinks, high-authority
